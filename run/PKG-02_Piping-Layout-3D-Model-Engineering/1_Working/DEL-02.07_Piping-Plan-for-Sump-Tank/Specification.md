## 1. Purpose and Scope

### 1.1 Purpose
This specification defines the requirements for the Piping Plan for Sump Tank drawing(s) to be developed as part of the Puget Sound Optimization Project (PSO) at Sumas Tank Farm. The drawing(s) shall clearly depict the sump tank, associated pumps, and all connected piping for the booster pump station and manifold area.

### 1.2 Scope
The Piping Plan for Sump Tank shall cover:
- Sump tank location and arrangement
- All piping connections to/from the sump tank
- Reinjection pump(s) and associated piping
- Dewatering equipment connections
- Drain piping from containment areas
- Vent piping as required
- Valve and instrument locations
- Pipe support reference points

### 1.3 Exclusions
- Detailed sump tank fabrication drawings (by tank vendor)
- Pump package internal piping (by vendor)
- Electrical and I&C wiring (covered by respective disciplines)

#### 2.1.2 Plan View Content
The plan view(s) shall include:

1. **Sump Tank**
   - Tank location with reference to site grid
   - Tank orientation and access provisions
   - All nozzle locations with size and orientation
   - Foundation/support references

2. **Piping Systems**
   - Drain piping from containment areas
   - Pump suction piping
   - Pump discharge piping (reinjection lines)
   - Vent piping
   - Dewatering system connections
   - Sample and drain connections

3. **Equipment**
   - Reinjection pump(s) location and orientation
   - Dewatering equipment location
   - Strainers/filters as applicable
   - Flow control devices

4. **Valves**
   - All valve locations with tag numbers
   - Valve types per P&ID requirements
   - Actuated valve identification

5. **Instrumentation**
   - Level instrumentation connections
   - Pressure instrumentation connections
   - Flow instrumentation connections (if applicable)
   - Local indication locations

6. **Supports and Access**
   - Pipe support location references
   - Access/maintenance provisions
   - Ladder/platform references (coordinate with structural)

#### 2.1.3 Sections and Elevations
Include as required to clarify:
- Vertical piping routing
- Pump elevations relative to tank
- Overflow arrangements
- Vent routing

#### 2.1.4 Details and Callouts
- Nozzle connection details (or reference to standard details)
- Special fittings or connections
- Instrument tap details
- Drain point details
