# Procedure: DEL-02.29 Study Report(s) (Piping)

## Purpose

### Objective
This procedure defines the steps for initiating, developing, reviewing, approving, and closing piping engineering study reports for the Puget Sound Optimization Project at Sumas Tank Farm.

### Scope
Applies to all piping engineering studies required during FEED and detailed design, including studies triggered by HAZOP actions, design reviews, TM requests, or unusual design conditions.

### Responsibilities
| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| Lead Piping Engineer | Study sponsorship, scope definition, approval, recommendation tracking |
| Study Author | Study development, analysis, report preparation |
| Peer Reviewer | Technical review of methodology, analysis, and conclusions |
| Checker | Independent verification of calculations |
| Document Controller | Document control, revision tracking, transmittal |
| Client (TM) | Review and approval (where required by scope) |
