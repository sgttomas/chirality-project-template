## 1. Scope

### 1.1 Purpose

This specification defines the requirements for the Regulatory Support Technical Package to be provided by the Engineering Contractor for the Puget Sound Optimization Project (PSO). The package comprises technical documentation and support activities provided to TM as requested throughout the project to facilitate regulatory compliance, permitting, and equipment certification.

### 1.2 Applicability

This specification applies to:
- Technical documentation for regulatory permit applications
- Stamped drawings and calculations for regulatory submissions
- Technical presentations to regulatory agencies and certifying authorities
- Support for Safety Codes Officer engagement
- Coordination of equipment registrations (CRN, ABSA, BCSA)
- Technical responses to regulatory queries

### 1.3 Boundaries

**In Scope:**
- Technical documentation preparation for regulatory use
- P.Eng. stamped drawings and calculations
- Technical support for regulatory meetings/presentations
- Equipment registration coordination with vendors
- Technical responses to regulatory authority queries
- Safety Codes Officer engagement support

**Out of Scope:**
- Regulatory application preparation (TM responsibility)
- Regulatory strategy development (TM responsibility)
- Environmental permitting (unless specifically requested)
- Commercial/administrative regulatory submissions
- Actual equipment registration (vendor responsibility)
