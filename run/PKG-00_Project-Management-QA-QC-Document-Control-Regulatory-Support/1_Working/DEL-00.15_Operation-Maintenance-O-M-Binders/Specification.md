## 1. Scope

### 1.1 Purpose

This specification defines the requirements for Operation & Maintenance (O&M) Binders to be compiled by the Engineering Contractor for the Puget Sound Optimization Project (PSO). O&M Binders provide comprehensive operational and maintenance documentation for equipment within project scope.

### 1.2 Applicability

This specification applies to:
- All new equipment within PSO scope at Sumas Tank Farm
- Equipment from all disciplines (mechanical, electrical, instrumentation)
- Documentation supporting equipment operation and maintenance

### 1.3 Boundaries

**In Scope:**
- Vendor manuals and documentation
- Operating instructions and procedures
- Maintenance procedures and schedules
- Relevant drawings and diagrams
- Spare parts lists and recommendations
- Equipment certificates and test records

**Out of Scope:**
- TM corporate operating procedures (TM responsibility)
- Site-wide maintenance programs (TM responsibility)
- Training program development (unless specified)
- Ongoing manual updates post-turnover (TM responsibility)
