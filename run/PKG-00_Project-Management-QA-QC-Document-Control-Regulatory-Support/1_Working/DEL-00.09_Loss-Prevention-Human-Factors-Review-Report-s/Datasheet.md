## Purpose
The Loss Prevention & Human Factors Review Reports document the findings, recommendations, and action items from LP and HF reviews conducted at specified design stages (e.g., 30/60/90% design completion). These reports demonstrate compliance with SOW requirements and track issues to closeout.

## Key Focus Areas

### Loss Prevention Focus Areas
| Area | PSO-Specific Considerations |
|------|----------------------------|
| Process Safety | Hydrocarbon containment, VS6 pump hazards, manifold integrity |
| Fire Protection | Pump area classification, detection/suppression coverage |
| Spill Prevention | New containment wall adequacy, drainage, leak detection |
| Mechanical Integrity | Pump/VFD reliability, valve integrity |
| Emergency Response | Access for emergency vehicles, shutdown provisions |

### Human Factors Focus Areas
| Area | PSO-Specific Considerations |
|------|----------------------------|
| Operability | Control interface for booster pumps/VFDs, blending control |
| Maintainability | Pump maintenance access, lifting provisions |
| Safety | Noise from pumps/VFDs, ergonomic hazards |
| Alarm Management | New alarms from booster station integration |
