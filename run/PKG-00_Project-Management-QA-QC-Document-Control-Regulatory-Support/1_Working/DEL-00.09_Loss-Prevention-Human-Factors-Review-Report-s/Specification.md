## 1. Purpose and Scope
This specification defines the requirements for the Loss Prevention and Human Factors Review Reports produced at specified design stages throughout the PSO project.

## 3. Requirements

### 3.1 General Report Requirements
| Req ID | Requirement | Source | Verification |
|--------|-------------|--------|--------------|
| SPEC-09.01 | Reports shall be produced at specified design milestones (FEED, 30% DD, 60% DD, 90% DD) | SOW-0460 | Inspection |
| SPEC-09.02 | Reports shall document all findings from LP reviews | SOW-0460 | Review |
| SPEC-09.03 | Reports shall document all findings from HF reviews | SOW-0460 | Review |
| SPEC-09.04 | Reports shall include action items with owners and due dates | SOW-0460 | Review |
| SPEC-09.05 | Reports shall track action closeout status | SOW-0460 | Review |
| SPEC-09.06 | Final report shall demonstrate all actions closed prior to IFC | SOW-0244 | Review |
| SPEC-09.07 | Reports shall include sections for: Executive Summary, Review Scope, Review Team, Methodology, LP Findings, HF Findings, Action Register, and Closeout Status | ASSUMPTION | Review |

### 3.2 Loss Prevention Content Requirements
| Req ID | Requirement | Source | Verification |
|--------|-------------|--------|--------------|
| SPEC-09.10 | LP sections shall address process safety considerations (hydrocarbon containment, pump hazards, manifold integrity) | SOW-0460 | Review |
| SPEC-09.11 | LP sections shall address fire protection adequacy (area classification, detection/suppression) | SOW-0460 | Review |
| SPEC-09.12 | LP sections shall address spill prevention/containment (containment wall, drainage, leak detection) | SOW-0460 | Review |
| SPEC-09.13 | LP sections shall address mechanical integrity concerns (pump/VFD reliability, valve integrity) | SOW-0460 | Review |
| SPEC-09.14 | LP sections shall address emergency response provisions (access, shutdown) | SOW-0460 | Review |

### 3.3 Human Factors Content Requirements
| Req ID | Requirement | Source | Verification |
|--------|-------------|--------|--------------|
| SPEC-09.20 | HF sections shall address operability considerations (control interface, blending control visibility) | SOW-0460 | Review |
| SPEC-09.21 | HF sections shall address maintainability provisions (pump access, lifting provisions) | SOW-0460 | Review |
| SPEC-09.22 | HF sections shall address safety/ergonomic factors (noise from pumps/VFDs, ergonomic hazards) | SOW-0460 | Review |
| SPEC-09.23 | HF sections shall address alarm management for new booster station alarms (if applicable) | SOW-0460 | Review |

### 3.4 Action Register Requirements
| Req ID | Requirement | Source | Verification |
|--------|-------------|--------|--------------|
| SPEC-09.30 | Each finding shall have a unique action ID (format: LP-### or HF-###) | ASSUMPTION | Inspection |
| SPEC-09.31 | Each action shall have an assigned owner | SOW-0460 | Review |
| SPEC-09.32 | Each action shall have a due date | ASSUMPTION | Review |
| SPEC-09.33 | Each action shall have a defined priority (Critical/High/Medium/Low) | ASSUMPTION | Review |
| SPEC-09.34 | Closed actions shall reference closeout evidence | SOW-0460 | Review |
| SPEC-09.35 | Action register shall include fields for: Action ID, Finding, Recommendation, Owner, Due Date, Priority, Status, and Closeout Evidence | ASSUMPTION | Review |

### 3.5 Documentation Requirements
| Req ID | Requirement | Source | Verification |
|--------|-------------|--------|--------------|
| SPEC-09.40 | Reports shall identify review team participants, roles, and qualifications | ASSUMPTION | Review |
| SPEC-09.41 | Reports shall identify review methodology/checklists used | ASSUMPTION | Review |
| SPEC-09.42 | Reports shall include or reference marked-up drawings | ASSUMPTION | Review |
| SPEC-09.43 | Reports shall be compatible with TM EDMS requirements | SOW-0441 | Inspection |
| SPEC-09.44 | Review team shall include: Risk Coordinator, LP Specialist, HF Specialist, Discipline Leads, Project Manager, and TM Representative (as applicable) | ASSUMPTION | Review |

## 4. Quality Requirements
| Req ID | Requirement | Source | Verification |
|--------|-------------|--------|--------------|
| SPEC-09.50 | Reports shall be reviewed internally by Project Manager prior to submission | SOW-0420 | Review |
| SPEC-09.51 | Reports shall be submitted to TM per DEL-00.08 Risk Management Plan schedule | SOW-0460 | Inspection |
| SPEC-09.52 | Closeout evidence shall be verifiable and specific (drawing revision, calculation, photo, or documented acceptance) | ASSUMPTION | Review |

## 5. Deliverable Format
- Report format: Microsoft Word (.docx) or PDF
- Action register format: Microsoft Excel (.xlsx)
- Naming convention: Per TM document numbering requirements (TBD)

## 6. Acceptance Criteria
| Criterion | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| AC-09.01 | All reviewed areas are documented |
| AC-09.02 | All findings have associated actions |
| AC-09.03 | All actions have owners and due dates |
| AC-09.04 | Closeout status is accurately reported |
| AC-09.05 | Final report shows all critical/high actions closed |
