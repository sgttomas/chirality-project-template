# Procedure: DEL-00.09 Loss Prevention & Human Factors Review Report(s)

## 1. Purpose
This procedure defines the steps for conducting LP and HF reviews and producing the associated reports at specified design milestones throughout the PSO project.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all Loss Prevention and Human Factors reviews conducted during FEED and Detailed Design phases for the PSO project at Sumas Tank Farm.

## 3. Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibilities |
|------|------------------|
| Risk Coordinator | Schedule reviews; coordinate logistics; track actions |
| LP Specialist | Lead LP review sessions; prepare LP findings sections |
| HF Specialist | Lead HF review sessions; prepare HF findings sections |
| Discipline Leads | Participate in reviews; address discipline-specific findings |
| Project Manager | Approve reports; ensure action closeout |
| TM Representative | Participate in reviews; accept final reports |

## 4. Procedure Steps

### 4.1 Review Preparation
| Step | Action | Responsible | Output |
|------|--------|-------------|--------|
| 4.1.1 | Identify upcoming review per DEL-00.08 Risk Management Plan schedule | Risk Coordinator | Review scheduled |
| 4.1.2 | Issue review meeting invitation (2+ weeks advance) | Risk Coordinator | Meeting invitation |
| 4.1.3 | Compile review package (drawings, P&IDs, 3D model, specs) | Discipline Leads | Review package |
| 4.1.4 | Distribute review package to participants (1+ week advance) | Risk Coordinator | Distribution record |
| 4.1.5 | Prepare review checklists | LP/HF Specialists | Checklists |
| 4.1.6 | Confirm participant attendance | Risk Coordinator | Attendance confirmation |

### 4.2 Loss Prevention Review Execution
| Step | Action | Responsible | Output |
|------|--------|-------------|--------|
| 4.2.1 | Open review session; confirm scope and objectives | LP Specialist | Session started |
| 4.2.2 | Review process safety provisions | LP Specialist + Team | Notes |
| 4.2.3 | Review fire protection provisions | LP Specialist + Team | Notes |
| 4.2.4 | Review spill prevention/containment provisions | LP Specialist + Team | Notes |
| 4.2.5 | Review mechanical integrity provisions | LP Specialist + Team | Notes |
| 4.2.6 | Review emergency response provisions | LP Specialist + Team | Notes |
| 4.2.7 | Summarize findings and agree on recommendations | LP Specialist + Team | Finding list |
| 4.2.8 | Assign actions, owners, and due dates | Project Manager | Action assignments |

### 4.3 Human Factors Review Execution
| Step | Action | Responsible | Output |
|------|--------|-------------|--------|
| 4.3.1 | Open review session; confirm scope and objectives | HF Specialist | Session started |
| 4.3.2 | Review operability provisions | HF Specialist + Team | Notes |
| 4.3.3 | Review maintainability provisions | HF Specialist + Team | Notes |
| 4.3.4 | Review safety/ergonomic provisions | HF Specialist + Team | Notes |
| 4.3.5 | Review alarm management provisions (if applicable) | HF Specialist + Team | Notes |
| 4.3.6 | Summarize findings and agree on recommendations | HF Specialist + Team | Finding list |
| 4.3.7 | Assign actions, owners, and due dates | Project Manager | Action assignments |

### 4.4 Report Preparation
| Step | Action | Responsible | Output |
|------|--------|-------------|--------|
| 4.4.1 | Draft executive summary per SPEC-09.07 | LP/HF Specialists | Summary draft |
| 4.4.2 | Document review scope and methodology per SPEC-09.07 | LP/HF Specialists | Scope section |
| 4.4.3 | Document review team and qualifications per SPEC-09.40, SPEC-09.44 | Risk Coordinator | Team section |
| 4.4.4 | Compile LP findings section per SPEC-09.10-09.14 | LP Specialist | LP findings |
| 4.4.5 | Compile HF findings section per SPEC-09.20-09.23 | HF Specialist | HF findings |
| 4.4.6 | Populate action register per SPEC-09.35 (Action ID, Finding, Recommendation, Owner, Due Date, Priority, Status, Closeout Evidence) | Risk Coordinator | Action register |
| 4.4.7 | Compile appendices (marked-up drawings, checklists) per SPEC-09.42 | Risk Coordinator | Appendices |
| 4.4.8 | Assemble complete report per SPEC-09.07 | Risk Coordinator | Draft report |
| 4.4.9 | Internal review per SPEC-09.50 | Project Manager | Review comments |
| 4.4.10 | Finalize report | Risk Coordinator | Final report |

### 4.5 Report Approval and Distribution
| Step | Action | Responsible | Output |
|------|--------|-------------|--------|
| 4.5.1 | Submit report to Project Manager for approval | Risk Coordinator | Submission |
| 4.5.2 | Project Manager reviews and approves | Project Manager | Approval |
| 4.5.3 | Submit report to TM (if required) | Project Manager | TM submission |
| 4.5.4 | Incorporate TM comments (if any) | Risk Coordinator | Revised report |
| 4.5.5 | Distribute approved report | Risk Coordinator | Distribution record |
| 4.5.6 | Upload to document control system | Risk Coordinator | EDMS record |

### 4.6 Action Tracking and Closeout
| Step | Action | Responsible | Output |
|------|--------|-------------|--------|
| 4.6.1 | Monitor action progress | Risk Coordinator | Status tracking |
| 4.6.2 | Follow up on overdue actions | Risk Coordinator | Follow-up record |
| 4.6.3 | Receive closeout evidence from action owners | Action Owners | Closeout evidence |
| 4.6.4 | Verify closeout evidence meets requirements per SPEC-09.52 | Risk Coordinator | Verification |
| 4.6.5 | Update action register status | Risk Coordinator | Updated register |
| 4.6.6 | Report closeout status at project meetings | Risk Coordinator | Meeting record |

### 4.7 Final Report Preparation (Pre-IFC)
| Step | Action | Responsible | Output |
|------|--------|-------------|--------|
| 4.7.1 | Compile closeout status for all prior reviews | Risk Coordinator | Closeout summary |
| 4.7.2 | Verify all critical/high actions are closed | Risk Coordinator | Verification |
| 4.7.3 | Document any accepted residual risks | Project Manager | Acceptance record |
| 4.7.4 | Prepare final LP/HF closeout summary | LP/HF Specialists | Closeout summary |
| 4.7.5 | Include in final report or as addendum | Risk Coordinator | Final report updated |
| 4.7.6 | Obtain final acceptance from TM | TM Representative | Acceptance record |

## 5. Review Schedule (ASSUMPTION)

| Milestone | Target Timing | Review Focus |
|-----------|---------------|--------------|
| FEED Review | End of FEED phase | Preliminary hazard identification |
| 30% DD Review | 30% Detailed Design | Layout and access assessment |
| 60% DD Review | 60% Detailed Design | Detailed safeguard/operability review |
| 90% DD Review | 90% Detailed Design | Final verification and closeout |
