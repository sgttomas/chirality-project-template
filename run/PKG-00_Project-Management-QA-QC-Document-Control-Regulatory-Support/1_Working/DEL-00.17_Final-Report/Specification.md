## 1. Scope

### 1.1 Purpose

This specification defines the requirements for the Final Report to be prepared by the Engineering Contractor for the Puget Sound Optimization Project (PSO). The Final Report shall provide a comprehensive compilation of final engineering documentation, summarizing the work performed, design decisions made, and deliverables produced during FEED and Detailed Design phases.

### 1.2 Applicability

This specification applies to:
- The Final Report document for PSO engineering work
- Summary content covering all disciplines (PKG-00 through PKG-09)
- Design basis, decisions, deviations, and recommendations

### 1.3 Boundaries

**In Scope:**
- FEED and Detailed Design engineering activities
- Design basis and criteria summary
- Discipline-by-discipline work summaries
- Key design decisions and deviations
- Recommendations for construction/commissioning

**Out of Scope:**
- Construction activities (covered in Supplemental Final Report DEL-00.18)
- Commissioning and startup support (covered in DEL-00.18)
- Detailed technical content (reference FD Package for details)
