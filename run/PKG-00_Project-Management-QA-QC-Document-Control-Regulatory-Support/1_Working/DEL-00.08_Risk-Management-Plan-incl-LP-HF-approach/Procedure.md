## 1. Purpose
This procedure defines the steps for developing, approving, and implementing the Risk Management Plan for the PSO project, including the approach to loss prevention and human factors reviews.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all risk management, loss prevention, and human factors activities across FEED and Detailed Design phases for the PSO project at Sumas Tank Farm.

## 3. Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibilities |
|------|------------------|
| Risk Coordinator | Develop and maintain plan; facilitate risk activities; track actions |
| Project Manager | Approve plan; chair risk reviews; escalate to TM |
| Discipline Leads | Participate in risk identification; own discipline-specific risks |
| LP Specialist | Lead loss prevention reviews; provide LP recommendations |
| HF Specialist | Lead human factors reviews; provide HF recommendations |
| TM Project Lead | Approve plan; participate in reviews; accept residual risk |

## 4. Procedure Steps

### 4.1 Plan Development
| Step | Action | Responsible | Output |
|------|--------|-------------|--------|
| 4.1.1 | Review SOW requirements for risk/LP/HF activities | Risk Coordinator | Requirements list |
| 4.1.2 | Obtain TM templates or standards (if applicable) | Risk Coordinator | Templates |
| 4.1.3 | Draft Risk Management Plan structure | Risk Coordinator | Draft outline |
| 4.1.4 | Define risk categories and evaluation criteria | Risk Coordinator | Risk matrix |
| 4.1.5 | Define LP review approach and schedule | LP Specialist | LP section draft |
| 4.1.6 | Define HF review approach and schedule | HF Specialist | HF section draft |
| 4.1.7 | Define roles and responsibilities | Risk Coordinator | RACI table |
| 4.1.8 | Compile complete draft plan | Risk Coordinator | Draft plan |
| 4.1.9 | Internal review and comment resolution | Project Manager | Reviewed draft |
| 4.1.10 | Submit to TM for review/agreement | Project Manager | Submission record |
| 4.1.11 | Incorporate TM comments | Risk Coordinator | Final plan |
| 4.1.12 | Obtain TM approval | TM Project Lead | Approved plan |

### 4.2 Risk Register Initialization
| Step | Action | Responsible | Output |
|------|--------|-------------|--------|
| 4.2.1 | Create risk register template per plan | Risk Coordinator | Blank register |
| 4.2.2 | Conduct initial risk identification workshop | Risk Coordinator + Discipline Leads | Initial risk list |
| 4.2.3 | Evaluate identified risks using matrix | Workshop participants | Rated risks |
| 4.2.4 | Assign risk owners and response strategies | Project Manager | Populated register |
| 4.2.5 | Define response actions and target dates | Risk Owners | Action plan |
| 4.2.6 | Distribute initial register | Risk Coordinator | Distribution record |

### 4.3 Risk Review Cycle
| Step | Action | Responsible | Frequency |
|------|--------|-------------|-----------|
| 4.3.1 | Collect risk status updates from owners | Risk Coordinator | Per plan (see Guidance) |
| 4.3.2 | Update risk register | Risk Coordinator | Per plan |
| 4.3.3 | Identify new risks | All team members | Ongoing |
| 4.3.4 | Present risk status at project meetings | Risk Coordinator | Weekly/bi-weekly |
| 4.3.5 | Escalate high/extreme risks to TM | Project Manager | As required |

### 4.4 Loss Prevention Review Execution
| Step | Action | Responsible | Output |
|------|--------|-------------|--------|
| 4.4.1 | Schedule LP review per plan timing | Risk Coordinator | Meeting invitation |
| 4.4.2 | Prepare review materials (drawings, P&IDs, specs) | Discipline Leads | Review package |
| 4.4.3 | Conduct LP review session | LP Specialist | Review notes |
| 4.4.4 | Document findings and recommendations | LP Specialist | Findings list |
| 4.4.5 | Assign actions and owners | Project Manager | Action assignments |
| 4.4.6 | Prepare LP Review Report | LP Specialist | DEL-00.09 input |
| 4.4.7 | Track actions to closure | Risk Coordinator | Updated register |

### 4.5 Human Factors Review Execution
| Step | Action | Responsible | Output |
|------|--------|-------------|--------|
| 4.5.1 | Schedule HF review per plan timing | Risk Coordinator | Meeting invitation |
| 4.5.2 | Prepare review materials (layouts, 3D model, procedures) | Discipline Leads | Review package |
| 4.5.3 | Conduct HF review session | HF Specialist | Review notes |
| 4.5.4 | Document findings and recommendations | HF Specialist | Findings list |
| 4.5.5 | Assign actions and owners | Project Manager | Action assignments |
| 4.5.6 | Prepare HF Review Report | HF Specialist | DEL-00.09 input |
| 4.5.7 | Track actions to closure | Risk Coordinator | Updated register |

### 4.6 Action Closeout
| Step | Action | Responsible | Output |
|------|--------|-------------|--------|
| 4.6.1 | Action owner completes required work | Action Owner | Completion evidence |
| 4.6.2 | Submit closeout package to Risk Coordinator | Action Owner | Closeout documentation |
| 4.6.3 | Verify closeout meets criteria per plan | Risk Coordinator | Verification record |
| 4.6.4 | Obtain approval for closure (if required) | Project Manager | Approval record |
| 4.6.5 | Update register status to Closed | Risk Coordinator | Updated register |

### 4.7 Escalation Process
| Step | Action | Responsible | Trigger |
|------|--------|-------------|---------|
| 4.7.1 | Identify risk requiring escalation | Risk Coordinator | Extreme/High risk, overdue action |
| 4.7.2 | Prepare escalation summary | Risk Coordinator | Escalation memo |
| 4.7.3 | Present to Project Manager | Risk Coordinator | PM decision |
| 4.7.4 | Escalate to TM if unresolved | Project Manager | TM notification |
| 4.7.5 | Document TM decision | Risk Coordinator | Decision record |
